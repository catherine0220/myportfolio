Assignment Overview

Payroll System Features

This system provides comprehensive payroll management functionality including:

  • Employee data management (add, edit, delete)
  • Salary calculation based on hours worked and pay rate
  • Automatic EPF (Employee Provident Fund) deductions
  • Medical plan deductions (Type A, B, or C)
  • Sales commission calculations
  • Net salary computation
  • Pay slip generation
  • Employee search functionality

Employee Types

Supports different employee types (Massage Therapist, Facialist, Manicurist) with varying pay rates and EPF contributions.

Automatic Calculations

Calculates basic salary, EPF deductions, medical deductions, sales commissions and net salary automatically.

Pay Slip Generation

Generates detailed pay slips showing all salary components and deductions in a clear format.

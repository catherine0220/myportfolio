Overview

Finexus Sdn Bhd, founded in 2000, is a Malaysian fintech company. It offers regulatory reporting, payment processing, and digital payment solutions through a modern and intuitive interface. As an intern, I actively participated in the design enhancement process, including converting the initial Adobe XD designs to Figma and improving the overall user experience.

Visit Finexus Website

My Role

As an intern, I played a key role in the UI/UX design process, from initial brainstorming to final implementation. I contributed to the creation of user personas, identified pain points, and designed wireframes and prototypes. Additionally, I was responsible for converting the project from Adobe XD to Figma, enhancing the design system, and ensuring consistency across all screens. I also participated in user testing to validate design decisions and improve the overall user journey.

Team Involved

The project involved a cross-functional team of designers, developers, and product managers.

As an intern, I collaborated closely with the team, contributing to design discussions, and assisting in the preparation of design assets for development. I learned how to effectively communicate design ideas to non-designers and how to incorporate feedback from multiple stakeholders into the final product.

Problem

The main problem we aimed to solve was the complexity of financial management for users. Many existing platforms were either too complicated or lacked essential features, leading to a poor user experience.

During the brainstorming phase, I helped identify key pain points through user research and persona creation, which guided the design process to ensure the platform was both functional and user-friendly.

Challenges

One of the biggest challenges was balancing functionality with simplicity. We needed to ensure the platform was powerful enough to handle complex financial tasks while remaining easy to use for non-technical users. I contributed by creating intuitive wireframes and prototypes to ensure it met both user and business goals.

Personal Takeaway

This project was a valuable learning experience for me as an intern. I gained hands-on experience in the entire design process, from research and ideation to wireframing and prototyping. I learned the importance of user-centered design and how to effectively collaborate with cross-functional teams to create a product that meets both user needs and business objectives.
